Our Story

Cozy Bean Café started as a dream to bring warm, handcrafted coffee and delicious meals to our community. Founded by two friends with a passion for quality and comfort, we opened our doors in 2020 and have been welcoming locals and visitors ever since.

We believe in using fresh, locally-sourced ingredients and creating a space where everyone feels at home—whether you're grabbing a quick espresso or settling in for a long chat.
